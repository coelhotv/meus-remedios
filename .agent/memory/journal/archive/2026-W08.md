### 2026-02-18 03:55 — Timezone Bug in Adherence Service [GRADUATED]
**Type:** BUG-FIX
**Files:** `src/utils/dateUtils.js`, `src/utils/adherenceLogic.js`, `src/services/api/adherenceService.js`
**Context:** Fixed HIGH priority issue from PR #50 review. Timezone inconsistency + duplicated isProtocolActiveOnDate.

- Symptom: Adherence calculations could be wrong for dates near midnight
